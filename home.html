This is my dog, Arlo

Arlo is a purebred mutt.

When I first got Arlo from the shelter, we were told he was a labrador retriever. After a DNA test, I learned that he is actually 40% pitbull terrier, 20% golden retreiver, and the rest is other random breeds.

Arlo has lots of energy.

He is not very good at fetch, but he loves to chase after small critters and his oversized cat toy.

Arlo loves his family.

Arlo always gets excited to go visit his (human) grandparents and loves to snuggle.

Arlo is not a very big fan of water.

Despite being a medium/large breed, Arlo does not really like to go anywhere near the water. I am slowly introducing him to the ocean, since he does like to walk down to the beach.
